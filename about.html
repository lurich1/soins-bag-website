Our Web3 Journey

Soncis_bags was born from a passion for education and empowerment, evolving into a Web3-powered social enterprise. We've embraced blockchain technology to create transparent, sustainable, and impactful fashion.

Our journey began with a mission to provide educational supplies to underprivileged children. Today, we're at the forefront of merging fashion with Web3 technology, producing high-quality, custom-made bags that carry a digital footprint of their impact.

Each Soncis_bag is more than just a fashion statement; it's a digital asset that represents your contribution to global education and sustainability efforts.

Our Web3 Vision & Mission

Vision

To lead the fusion of fashion and Web3 technology, transforming lives through education support, transparent supply chains, and community-driven initiatives.

Mission

  • Support Education through NFT Initiatives
  • Implement Transparent, Ethical Production
  • Create Innovative, Tech-Embedded Bags
  • Establish a Circular Economy Model
  • Build a Global, Decentralized Community
