Our Initiatives

Eco Eddie

Meet Eco Eddie, our sustainability mascot! Eddie represents our commitment to eco-friendly practices. Through Eddie, we educate our community about the importance of sustainable living and how our products contribute to a greener future.

KAAYA

KAAYA (Knapsacks And Accessories Yielding Awareness) is our social initiative. For every bag sold, we donate a portion to provide school supplies to underprivileged children, ensuring that education reaches every corner of the world.
